// FEATURES

Your phone's silent guardian

Everything you need to know who's touching your device — without them ever knowing they've been caught.

Silent Photo Capture

When a wrong PIN, pattern, or password is entered, the front camera silently snaps a photo — no shutter sound, no flash, no warning.

CORE FEATURE

Instant Notifications

Get an immediate alert with the captured image attached — so you know exactly who tried to access your phone, even when you're away.

REAL-TIME

Anti-Touch Detection

Detects when someone picks up or moves your phone without unlocking it, triggering an alarm and capturing a photo automatically.

MOTION SENSOR

Charger Removal Alert

Someone unplugged your phone from charging? Intruder Eye snaps a photo and sounds an alarm — ideal for charging in public spaces.

SMART ALERT

Photo History Log

Every captured intruder photo is saved in a private gallery with timestamps. Review, share, or delete the evidence at any time.

EVIDENCE LOG

Loud Alarm Trigger

Plays a loud alarm when an intruder is detected, scaring them off and drawing attention — your first line of defense, making noise when it counts.

// HOW IT WORKS

Set it up in seconds

Three simple steps and your phone is protected around the clock.

01

Install & Enable

Download Intruder Eye from the Play Store, open the app, and grant Device Administrator permission. Takes under a minute.

02

Configure Your Triggers

Set how many wrong unlock attempts trigger a capture, enable motion detection, and toggle charger removal alerts to your preference.

03

Lock & Walk Away

Lock your phone normally. Intruder Eye runs silently in the background. Any unauthorized access attempt is captured and you're notified instantly.
